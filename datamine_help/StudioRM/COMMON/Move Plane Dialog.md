# Move Plane

### To access this dialog:

  * Using the **[command line](<Command_Toolbar.md>)** , enter "move-plane".

  * Use the quick key combination "mpl".

  * Display the **[Find Command](<findcommand.md>)** screen, locate **move-plane** and click **Run**.

Shift the current view plane either forward or backward, maintaining any clipping limits already specified.

Enter the Distance to Move Plane (in metric or imperial units, depending on your global [options](<Options.md>)) to move the current view plane. 

You can enter a positive value to move the view plane forwards along the line of view, or backwards with a negative value.
