# Plots Window General Concepts

The [Plots](<>) window, designed for the preparation of formal reporting of mining data, necessarily takes a different approach to design, compared to 3D windows.

As a collection of potentially printable items, a plot sheet is fundamentally 2-dimensional, but can represent 3D data within a projection, and potentially as a slice of data that coincides with a section. See [Sections and Projections](<alignviewwithsection.md>).
