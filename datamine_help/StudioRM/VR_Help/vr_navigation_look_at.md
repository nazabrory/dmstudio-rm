# Look At

The **Look At** Mode button is found on the **3D View** ribbon and is used to:

  * Indicate that **Look At Mode** is currently set,

  * Toggle off this mode,

  * Set **Floating Viewpoint + Look At Mode** using a selected point on a 3D Object,

  * Look at a selected point when in **Fixed Viewpoint \+ View From Inside** modes.

During Look At mode, the camera orients to view the selected data directly, and if an animation is playing, remains fixed on that point throughout.

To set Floating Viewpoint + Look At Mode:

  1. If in **Floating Viewpoint** or **Fixed Viewpoint + View From Outside** modes,

  2. Click **Look At**.

  3. In the 3D window, select (click on) a 3D object, or a point on an object.

To cancel Look At mode:

  * Click **Look At** again to disable this mode.
